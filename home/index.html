About Me

I am a PhD student in the Finance PhD program in Stockholm School of Economics starting this fall.

My current research interests are behavioral finance, financial intermediaries and corporate finance (potentially).

Grew up in Hangzhou, China and obtained the Bachelor of Finance (Honours) degree with the University Medal from the Australian National University in Canberra, Australia. I love soccer, reading and playing Go and I am still in the process of learning piano.
